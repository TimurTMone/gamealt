FAQ
Common questions
Is this a replacement for speech therapy? ▼
No. BabyPath is a supplement to professional therapy, not a replacement. If your child has significant delays, please see a certified speech-language pathologist. Our tool helps you practice at home between sessions and gives you evidence-based techniques to use daily.
Will using 3 languages confuse my child? ▼
No. Research consistently shows bilingualism does NOT cause speech delays (Petitto et al., 2001; ASHA position statement). Count vocabulary across ALL languages. A child who says 10 words in English and 15 in Russian has 25 words — that's great!
My child is 2 and barely talks. Should I worry? ▼
Many "late talkers" catch up by age 3. But early intervention is most effective before age 3. If your child has fewer than 50 words by 24 months or isn't combining words, it's worth getting an evaluation. Trust your instincts — you know your child best. Our milestone tracker can help you identify areas to discuss with your pediatrician.
Is this just for moms? ▼
Absolutely not. This was built by a dad. Research shows children benefit enormously when fathers are actively involved in language development. Every feature is designed for both parents. No pink-washing, no "mommy" language — just parenting.
What makes this different from other apps? ▼
Three things: (1) Every word has a parent coaching tip telling you HOW to use it naturally — not just "tap to hear." (2) We tell you what approximations to accept — "ba" for ball IS a word. (3) We support underserved languages (Russian, Kyrgyz) that most apps ignore.
