Phrases to Model
  • “Time to eat!”

How to Use These Cards
BabyPath • Turn Every Moment Into a Language Lesson
  • Print the cards you need. Cut if needed. Tape them where you use them — kitchen, bathroom, bedroom door.
  • Glance at the card during the routine. Pick 1–2 target words to focus on each week.
  • Use any language — or all three! Multilingual input helps, not hurts.
